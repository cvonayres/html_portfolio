Summary

I am a 41 year old engineer, with a life long love of learning. Over the years I have designed cutting edge machine, lead corss-functional high performance teams and oversaw multple million pound projects.

However with a advent of XR my attention has being brought back to software development [via PLC > industry XR > game dev > software] and the simple beauty that comes from elegent code. It has also highlighted the stagnentcy found in so many engineering teams/compony who want to do what works and not push for better.

I originally started software with C++ and Fortran back in mid 90's and along the way have learned Root [from CERN], Python and Labview. However with getting into Game Dev with unreal and C++ my new journey started, since then I have learned Rust [love it], Go [really like it for web] and have started this course to sure up my front-end skills and learn all about web development with the hope that I can transfer my soft skills [i.e. project, engineering management, etc] into the software scope and get a full time job doing what I love and working with people who want to push what can be done.
